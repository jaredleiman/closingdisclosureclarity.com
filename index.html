Common questions.

What is a Closing Disclosure?expand_more

A Closing Disclosure is the federally required 5-page document your lender must provide at least 3 business days before closing. It contains every fee, charge, and credit associated with your mortgage — and it's legally binding.

How quickly will I receive my analysis?expand_more

Most reports are delivered to your inbox within minutes of uploading your document. Your 3-day review window is short — we built this system to work fast.

Is this legal advice?expand_more

No. Closing Disclosure Clarity provides general educational analysis — not legal advice, not financial advice. If a fee appears problematic, we provide plain-English context and suggested questions for your lender.

Is my document secure?expand_more

Your document is transmitted securely via encrypted upload and used solely to generate your analysis. We do not store, share, or sell your personal financial documents.
